NIFTY Pivot Gap 3.0 Specs & Logic Rules

Setup & Gap Conditions

  • Gap Requirement: Opening gap must be greater than > 30 pts (Gap Up) or less than < -30 pts (Gap Down).
  • Directional Rule:
    • If Gap Up: Market open price must be above Pivot level (P).
    • If Gap Down: Market open price must be below Pivot level (P).
  • No Trade on Flat Opens: If opening gap is within ±30 points, Strategy 1 is completely skipped.

Entry Trigger & Logic

  • Pivot Touch: Spot price must touch or cross the Pivot level (P) before 13:00 cutoff.
  • P Tolerance: A margin of ±10 points around P counts as a valid touch.
  • Delayed Entry: When touch is detected, it waits for the current 1-minute candle to close + 2 seconds buffer (e.g. entry at candle_end + 2s) to filter out fake breaks.
  • Lots & Expiry: Buys 2 Lots (130 shares) of ATM Call (CE) for Gap Up or ATM Put (PE) for Gap Down.

Target, SL & Exit

  • CE Target: R1 Pivot level (Spot).
  • CE Stop Loss: Pivot level (P) minus 20 points (P - 20).
  • PE Target: S1 Pivot level (Spot).
  • PE Stop Loss: Pivot level (P) plus 20 points (P + 20).
  • EOD Force Exit: If target or SL is not hit by 15:20, the strategy auto-exits at market premium.

Setup & Gap Conditions

  • Any Gap Day: Requires opening gap greater than > 30 pts (Gap Up) or less than < -30 pts (Gap Down).
  • No Directional Bias: Runs same trade regardless of whether gap is up or down.
  • No Pivot Touch Required: Operates entirely independently of the Pivot level (P) touch.

Entry Trigger & Strikes

  • Immediate Entry: Automatically fires orders exactly at 09:16:02 on all valid gap days.
  • Legs & Strikes: Shorts a symmetric ATM ± 100 strangle:
    • Shorts 1 Lot (65 shares) of ATM + 100 CE.
    • Shorts 1 Lot (65 shares) of ATM - 100 PE.
  • Lots: 1 Lot each (Total margin blocked: ~₹70,000 - ₹80,000 depending on broker).

Target, SL & Exit

  • Combined SL Limit: Total M2M combined loss across CE and PE premium is capped at ₹7,000.
  • Live Monitoring: Evaluates combined P&L in real-time: (ce_entry - ce_ltp + pe_entry - pe_ltp) * 65.
  • SL Trigger: If combined loss hits or exceeds -₹7,000, both legs are closed simultaneously at market.
  • EOD Force Exit: If combined SL is not hit, the strangle is squared off at 15:20 to collect theta decay.

Safety Rules

  • Thursday Expiry Skip: Both strategies are strictly disabled on Thursday to prevent 0DTE options decay and gamma risk.
  • Max Daily Loss Limits: Buying is limited by P±20 spot SL. Selling is protected by the combined ₹7,000 SL.
  • Execution Modes: Switch between Paper Trade (Simulated Orders) and Live Execution (Angel One via OpenAlgo) inside the config panel.

Backtest & Historical ROI

  • Option Buying (BASE): 1-year backtest profit of +₹1,11,787 (using 2-lot premium ROI of 721%).
  • Option Selling (Strangle): 1-year backtest profit of +₹1,16,100 (approx. 145-193% on margin).
  • Combined Profit: Net profit of +₹2,27,887 over 252 trading days in 2025-2026.
